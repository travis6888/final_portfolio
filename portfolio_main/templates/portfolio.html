BookIt

Trying to plan events can be time consuming. BookIt helps you find events that fit your schedule. BookIt is synced with Google Calendar and searches for events via meetup, eventbrite, and more to get events based on the hours you are free and the activities you choose! Invite your friends and family to find events that work for both of your schedules too!

HobbySwap

Look in your garage or shed! I bet you have some unused expensive hobby items. I know I have quite a few. HobbySwap makes it easy to rent out those hobby items. While also allowing others to try new hobbies or use some of best equipment for a fraction of the cost. Similar to the other sharing sites out there like AirBnb or RelayRides.
